Custom Cards for Legends of Runeterra

This section is for one of my personal projects for Game Design and Collaborative Work. The work that I have done on this page is for "Legends of Runeterra" by Riot Games. This card game takes place in the world of League of Legends and expands on the world through cards and its characters. I will be adding more custom cards later down the line, and will post my finished products here on this page and on my Instagram. The cards that are one this page are finished products.

DISCLAIMER: These cards are not offical works of Riot games, nor am I associated with Riot Games. I am simply making cards for a company and game that I very much enjoy, as well as showcase what I can do in terms of creative work for taking characters form LoL and moving them to card-form. Links to the images and references I've used in this project will be below.
